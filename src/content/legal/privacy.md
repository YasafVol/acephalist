This Privacy Policy explains how Acephalist handles information when you use this website.

#### What this site collects

Most of the public site can be viewed without creating an account or submitting personal information.

The site currently may process:

- basic technical request data that your browser sends when visiting pages
- browser storage used for preferences such as theme selection
- browser storage used by utility pages such as drafts or note versions
- note content and related metadata if you choose to use the authenticated utility features
- information you choose to send through email or third-party social platforms linked from this site

#### Utility features

Some utility pages support note creation, sharing, or saving. If you use those features, the site may store note content, note titles, summaries, tags, timestamps, visibility settings, and share tokens in services used to operate the site.

If you sign in to use protected utility features, authentication is handled through the site's authentication provider. Basic account identifiers needed to authorize those features may be processed for access control.

#### How information is used

Information is used only to:

- render and operate the site
- remember local preferences
- provide utility features you choose to use
- secure the site and limit unauthorized access
- respond if you contact the site owner directly

#### Cookies and local storage

This site may use browser storage, including local storage, to remember preferences and support utility features. It is not currently configured with advertising cookies.

#### Sharing

Information is not sold. Data may be processed by hosting, storage, authentication, and infrastructure providers to operate the site. If you follow external links or contact the site owner through third-party services, those services apply their own privacy practices.

#### Retention

Locally stored data remains in your browser until you clear it or the application overwrites it. Data submitted through site utility features may be retained as long as needed to operate those features, maintain shared links, or manage the service.

#### Your choices

You can stop using the site at any time, clear local browser storage from your device, and avoid using optional utility or sharing features if you do not want related data processed.

#### Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.
